Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about renting vehicles with us.

1. How do I book a vehicle?

Search for vehicles on our homepage, select your preferred vehicle, choose rental dates, and complete the booking form with your details and payment information.

2. What are the age requirements for renting?

You must be at least 21 years old and hold a valid driver’s license. Additional fees may apply for drivers under 25.

3. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed per our Refund Policy. Check the policy for deadlines and potential fees.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards and select digital wallets. Payment is required at booking.

5. Is insurance included?

Basic insurance is included, but you can purchase additional coverage during booking. Review terms at checkout.

6. How do I contact support?

Email us at support@vehiclerental.com or call our support line at 1-800-123-4567.

7. Can I modify my booking?

Yes, log in to your account to modify dates or vehicle selection, subject to availability.

8. What happens if the vehicle is damaged?

You’re responsible for damages during your rental period, subject to our insurance terms. Report issues immediately.
