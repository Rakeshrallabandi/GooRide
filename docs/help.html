Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

To book a vehicle, browse vehicles on the home page, click on a vehicle card to view details, and click the "Book Now" button if the vehicle is available. Follow the prompts to confirm your booking.

Go to "My Posts" from the user menu, click "Add a Vehicle," and fill out the form with vehicle details, images, and pricing. Submit the form to list your vehicle.

Liking a vehicle adds it to your "Likes" page for quick access. Saving a vehicle adds it to your "Saved" page, allowing you to bookmark vehicles you’re interested in renting later.
